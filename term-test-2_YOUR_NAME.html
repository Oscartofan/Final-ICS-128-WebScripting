Exercise 2

Professor Brandon never imagined that his life as a computer science professor at the Camosun College would take such a fantastical turn.

As he stood at the lectern, explaining the intricacies of jQuery and communicating with web servers to his students, a peculiar sensation washed over him. Suddenly, lines of arcane symbols began to materialize before his eyes, intertwining with the code on his slides.

Bewildered, he watched as his power-point clicker levitated out of his hand and his laptop emitted sparks of magical energy.

With a mixture of shock and curiosity, Professor Brandon realized that he wasn't just a mere mortal, he was a wizard.

And so began his journey into a world where binary code and incantations intertwined, where the realms of technology and magic merged into a fascinating tapestry of knowledge and power.
